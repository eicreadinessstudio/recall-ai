The problem

Most organisations have documents, but not governed operational knowledge. Critical know-how is spread across files, systems, tickets, and individual experts — and usually breaks exactly where context, traceability, and judgment matter most.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the workarounds, edge-case judgment, and operational memory built over years of practice. In most companies, that knowledge was never truly captured.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit documents, but not the reasoning behind them. The gap between written procedure and actual operational practice remains hidden until work breaks.

Slow onboarding · Repeated relearning

Answers that stay fragmented

The answer may already exist — in a PDF, a Jira ticket, a config export, or a wiki page. Without governed linking and validation, it remains scattered, ambiguous, and hard to trust.

Operational risk · Search friction

Site logic

Main site for concept, separate site for demo

requivo.at should explain the category, the problem, and the intended system logic. demo.requivo.at should remain a separate, clearly labelled concept demo environment.

Naming

Use “concept demo”, not “product walkthrough”

Because the product does not yet exist in final form, the language should stay careful and factual: concept demo, illustrative interface, future workflow logic.

Design direction

White, sharp, precise, enterprise

The page now uses a lighter Palantir-like structure: strong typography, white background, clean borders, restrained accents, and interface-led credibility rather than abstract deep-tech decoration.

Use cases

Requivo is relevant wherever operational expertise is critical, distributed, and difficult to preserve — especially where fragmented sources and weak documentation create risk.

Operations & Manufacturing

Preserve plant procedures, engineering know-how, and maintenance expertise before critical experience gaps become operational disruption.

Enterprise & Professional Services

Reduce time spent searching across fragmented systems and make internal expertise easier to access, validate, and reuse when it is needed.

Regulated environments

Traceability, source linkage, and structured validation make Requivo suitable for quality, compliance, and safety-relevant workflows.
