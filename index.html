AI platform for capturing and retrieving operational know-how

Critical know-how disappears every day.

The product

Two core workflows. One operational knowledge layer.

Requivo helps organisations capture operational know-how, preserve expert knowledge, and make it accessible when and where it is needed.

Knowledge capture

Capture operational knowledge from documents, procedures, and expert input.

Expert routing

Route questions to the right expertise when the answer is not yet documented.

The result is a reusable operational knowledge layer that reduces repeated questions, shortens ramp-up time, and protects critical expertise from being lost.

The problem

Knowledge fragmentation

Large organisations operate across hundreds of systems, making critical information difficult to locate.

Time lost searching

Employees spend a significant portion of their workweek searching for internal documentation and data.

Expertise leaving organisations

When experienced employees retire or change roles, organisations lose valuable operational knowledge.

Use cases

Manufacturing

Capture engineering knowledge, plant procedures and operational expertise across manufacturing environments.

Enterprise operations

Reduce time spent searching for internal knowledge across systems and documentation.

Knowledge retention

Preserve institutional expertise before experienced employees leave the organisation.
