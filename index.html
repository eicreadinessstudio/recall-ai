The problem

It's not a documentation problem.
It's a knowledge problem.

Most organisations confuse the two. Document management tools capture what people choose to write. They cannot capture what was never written — and never will be.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the judgment, the workarounds, and the exceptions they carried for decades. No existing system captures what was never written down.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit the manual. Not the judgment. The gap between documented procedure and how work actually gets done is never recorded — and every new hire rediscovers it from scratch.

Lost productivity · Infinite restart

Answers that exist but can't be found

In any large organisation, the answer to almost any operational question already exists — in someone's head, two floors up or two time zones away. Without a routing system, it stays there.

Operational risk · Repeated cost
The product

Two workflows.
One operational knowledge layer.

Requivo helps organisations capture operational know-how, preserve expert knowledge, and make it accessible when and where it is needed.

01 — Capture

Knowledge Capture

Capture operational knowledge from documents, procedures, and expert input.

02 — Route

Expert Routing

Route questions to the right expertise when the answer is not yet documented.

The result is a reusable operational knowledge layer that reduces repeated questions, shortens ramp-up time, and protects critical expertise from being lost.
